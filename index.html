WELCOME TO SHOOT SIMPLE

Shoot simple is the fast, affordable way to get professional property images for estate agents.

The premise is simple, you take the photos, we edit them and then send them back next working day. No photography training is needed as we'll send you a 'How to Shoot' guide when you sign up. When you send us a set of images we'll then enhance the colors, straighten them, brighten them up and add blue skies if it was cloudy. To send them over simply login, drag your photos onto our upload page and click upload. You'll be able to download the edited images the next working day. And that's it!

Shoot simple is the brain child of two property photographers based in Kent. With a combined experience of over a decade in property photography we realised that the real skill in getting the best images for properties was in the editing. Creating Shoot Simple meant that we could offer professional level images, in a fraction of the time, for a fraction of the price.

To keep the highest quality levels, we make sure all editors have to go through our intensive training program so they have the knowledge to get the very best from your images.

Our biggest focus is on making the process as simple for you, so you can get great images on the market, as quickly as possible. If there is anything you think we could add or improve on let us know. We’re always happy to hear ways we can make your life easier.
